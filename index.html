Intro

Welcome! This site is a working example of a business landing page hosted entirely on the AWS cloud. With blazing-fast content delivery via CloudFront and scalable storage through Amazon S3, your website can perform at an enterprise level—without enterprise-level costs.

This temporary demo shows how we can take a sleek, custom layout and deploy it globally in minutes. Everything you see is version-controlled through GitHub and auto-deployed using GitHub Actions.

About Us

Rick’s Business is focused on delivering high-quality solutions with a professional, approachable style. Whether it's a product showcase, service offering, or a simple contact portal—this site gives a live example of what we can create for your brand.

This layout is customizable, mobile-friendly, and backed by reliable AWS infrastructure. As your needs grow, the platform grows with you—whether you need e-commerce, media galleries, or blog content integration.
